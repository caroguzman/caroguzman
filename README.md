### Full Stack Developer - ecommerce
This project is an ecommerce aimed at people interested in ordering Asian gastronomy online, focusing mainly on sushi. When we began planning this project, we set out to achieve in a simple and fast way that the client can place an order for his favorite food, as well as make it easier for the owner of the business to communicate his product, publish offers and manage orders. in an orderly manner. In this application, registered users can browse the available products, create an order to their liking with the products they prefer, or choose one of the assembled tables. You can also plan the way and date of delivery of your order.

Repo: https://github.com/jcamblong/grupo_4_genkisushi
